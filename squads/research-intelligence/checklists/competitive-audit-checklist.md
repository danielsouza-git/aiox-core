# Competitive Audit Checklist

Quality checklist specific to competitive audits and visual benchmarking.

## Competitor Coverage

- [ ] **Minimum 5 competitors** - At least 5 competitors analyzed
- [ ] **Competitor classification** - Each categorized as direct, indirect, or aspirational
- [ ] **Competitor data** - Founding year, size, funding, HQ documented
- [ ] **URLs documented** - All competitor URLs and social profiles captured
- [ ] **Mix of sizes** - Includes both larger and smaller competitors

## Audit Dimensions

- [ ] **Visual identity covered** - Logo, colors, typography, imagery assessed
- [ ] **Messaging covered** - Taglines, value props, tone documented
- [ ] **Positioning covered** - Market position and differentiation noted
- [ ] **Digital presence covered** - Website, social, content assessed
- [ ] **Pricing intelligence** - Pricing models and tiers noted (where available)

## Visual Evidence

- [ ] **Screenshots captured** - Key pages documented visually
- [ ] **Color palettes extracted** - Hex values provided
- [ ] **Typography identified** - Font families named
- [ ] **Capture dated** - Date of capture noted for freshness
- [ ] **Consistent viewport** - Same device/resolution for comparison

## Scoring & Comparison

- [ ] **Scoring criteria defined** - Clear 1-10 scale with definitions
- [ ] **Consistent application** - Same criteria applied to all competitors
- [ ] **Comparison matrix** - Side-by-side table with all competitors
- [ ] **Weighted scores** - Dimension weights applied
- [ ] **Ranking provided** - Competitors ranked overall

## Strategic Analysis

- [ ] **Differentiation opportunities** - Minimum 3 identified
- [ ] **Competitive threats** - Key threats flagged
- [ ] **White space identified** - Unoccupied positions noted
- [ ] **Pattern analysis** - Common industry patterns documented
- [ ] **Recommendations actionable** - Specific, implementable suggestions

## Quality Standards

- [ ] **Sources documented** - All URLs and data sources cited
- [ ] **No subjective claims** - Opinions labeled as assessor judgment
- [ ] **Evidence-based** - Each rating backed by specific evidence
- [ ] **Recency** - All data current (< 6 months)
- [ ] **Spell-checked** - No errors in competitor names or data

---

## Scoring

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 90-100% | Comprehensive | Ready for strategic use |
| 75-89% | Strong | Minor gaps to fill |
| 60-74% | Adequate | Additional research needed |
| Below 60% | Insufficient | Major gaps, redo sections |
