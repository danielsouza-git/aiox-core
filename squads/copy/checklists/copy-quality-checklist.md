# Copy Quality Checklist

Universal quality checklist for all copy types.

## Clarity & Readability

- [ ] **Single idea per sentence** - Each sentence conveys one clear thought
- [ ] **Active voice** - Verbs are active, not passive (80%+ active)
- [ ] **Simple words** - No jargon unless necessary for audience
- [ ] **Short paragraphs** - Max 3-4 lines per paragraph
- [ ] **Scannable structure** - Headers, bullets, whitespace used effectively
- [ ] **Reading level** - Appropriate for target audience (typically 6-8th grade)

## Voice & Tone

- [ ] **Brand voice match** - Copy sounds like the brand
- [ ] **Consistent tone** - Same tone throughout the piece
- [ ] **Appropriate formality** - Matches context and audience
- [ ] **Natural flow** - Reads like human speech, not corporate speak
- [ ] **No forbidden words** - Checked against brand vocabulary bank

## Persuasion Elements

- [ ] **Clear benefit** - Reader knows what's in it for them
- [ ] **Proof elements** - Claims supported by evidence, data, or testimonials
- [ ] **Urgency/scarcity** - Where appropriate, motivates action
- [ ] **Objection handling** - Addresses likely concerns
- [ ] **Social proof** - Includes relevant credibility markers

## Call to Action

- [ ] **Single primary CTA** - One clear next step
- [ ] **Action verb** - CTA starts with a verb
- [ ] **Specific outcome** - Reader knows what happens when they click
- [ ] **Low friction** - CTA is easy to complete
- [ ] **Visible placement** - CTA is prominent and easy to find

## Technical Quality

- [ ] **No spelling errors** - Spell-checked thoroughly
- [ ] **No grammar errors** - Grammar verified
- [ ] **Consistent formatting** - Same style throughout
- [ ] **Proper punctuation** - Commas, periods, quotes used correctly
- [ ] **No broken links** - All links tested (if applicable)

## Compliance & Ethics

- [ ] **No false claims** - All statements are truthful
- [ ] **Proper disclaimers** - Required legal language included
- [ ] **No manipulative tactics** - Ethical persuasion only
- [ ] **Accessibility** - Copy accessible to all readers
- [ ] **Cultural sensitivity** - No offensive content

## Final Checks

- [ ] **Read aloud test** - Copy sounds natural when spoken
- [ ] **Mobile preview** - Looks good on small screens
- [ ] **Fresh eyes review** - Someone else has reviewed it
- [ ] **Matches brief** - Delivers what was requested
- [ ] **Ready for delivery** - All revisions incorporated

---

## Scoring

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 90-100% | Excellent | Ready for delivery |
| 75-89% | Good | Minor revisions needed |
| 60-74% | Fair | Significant revisions needed |
| Below 60% | Poor | Major rewrite required |
