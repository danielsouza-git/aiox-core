# Copy Frameworks Reference

Comprehensive guide to copywriting frameworks used by the Copy Squad.

---

## Conversion Frameworks

### AIDA
**Attention → Interest → Desire → Action**

Best for: Landing pages, ads, short-form sales copy

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Attention | Stop the scroll | Bold headline, surprising stat |
| Interest | Build engagement | Relevant problem, intriguing angle |
| Desire | Create want | Benefits, transformation, proof |
| Action | Drive conversion | Clear CTA, urgency |

```
Attention: "97% of landing pages fail to convert. Here's why."
Interest: "Most businesses focus on features instead of benefits..."
Desire: "Imagine doubling your conversion rate in 30 days..."
Action: "Get the free Landing Page Audit → "
```

---

### PAS
**Problem → Agitation → Solution**

Best for: Pain-focused copy, problem-aware audiences

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Problem | Identify the pain | State the issue clearly |
| Agitation | Amplify the pain | Consequences of not solving |
| Solution | Present your answer | Your offer as the fix |

```
Problem: "Struggling to find qualified leads?"
Agitation: "Every day without leads is revenue lost. Your competitors are closing deals while you're chasing dead ends..."
Solution: "Our Lead Engine delivers 50+ qualified leads monthly, guaranteed."
```

---

### PASTOR
**Problem → Amplify → Story → Transform → Offer → Response**

Best for: Long-form sales pages, webinars, VSLs

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Problem | Identify their pain | Name the specific struggle |
| Amplify | Make it worse | Show consequences |
| Story | Share relevant narrative | Your journey or client story |
| Transform | Show the outcome | Before/after contrast |
| Offer | Present your solution | What they get |
| Response | Call to action | What to do now |

---

### BAB
**Before → After → Bridge**

Best for: Transformation stories, case studies, testimonials

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Before | Current painful state | "You're working 60-hour weeks..." |
| After | Desired future state | "Imagine working 30 hours with 2x income..." |
| Bridge | How to get there | "Our system makes this possible..." |

---

### 4Ps
**Promise → Picture → Proof → Push**

Best for: Direct response, quick conversions

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Promise | Big claim | "Double your email list in 30 days" |
| Picture | Paint the outcome | "Wake up to 100 new subscribers daily" |
| Proof | Back it up | Testimonials, data, results |
| Push | Drive action | "Start your free trial now" |

## Content Frameworks

### HCEA
**Hook → Context → Entrega → Action**

Best for: Social posts, captions, short content

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Hook | Stop the scroll | Surprising statement, question |
| Context | Set the stage | Background, relevance |
| Entrega | Deliver value | The main insight or tip |
| Action | Next step | Like, comment, click, save |

```
Hook: "I deleted 80% of my content strategy."
Context: "After 5 years of posting daily, I realized something..."
Entrega: "Quality beats quantity. One viral post > 100 ignored ones."
Action: "Save this if you're ready to work smarter, not harder."
```

---

### StoryBrand (SB7)
**Hero → Problem → Guide → Plan → Action → Success → Failure**

Best for: Brand messaging, website copy, brand storytelling

| Element | Your Customer | Your Brand |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Hero | The protagonist | Not you - them |
| Problem | What they face | External, internal, philosophical |
| Guide | Expert helper | You, with empathy + authority |
| Plan | Clear steps | Simple 3-step process |
| Action | The call | Direct or transitional |
| Success | Win scenario | Life after solving |
| Failure | Loss scenario | Stakes if they don't act |

---

### QUEST
**Qualify → Understand → Educate → Stimulate → Transition**

Best for: Educational content, lead magnets, nurture sequences

| Stage | Purpose | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Qualify | Filter audience | "If you're a B2B founder..." |
| Understand | Show empathy | "You know the struggle of..." |
| Educate | Teach something | Share valuable insight |
| Stimulate | Create desire | Show what's possible |
| Transition | Bridge to offer | Lead to CTA |

## Email Frameworks

### 1-2-3 Formula
**One idea → Two benefits → Three proofs**

Best for: Newsletters, value-focused emails

```
ONE IDEA: The power of compound consistency
TWO BENEFITS:
- You'll build momentum that's hard to stop
- You'll see exponential results over time
THREE PROOFS:
1. James Clear wrote Atomic Habits this way
2. Our clients average 47% better retention
3. Even 1% daily = 37x yearly improvement
```

---

### Star-Chain-Hook
**Star (attention) → Chain (logic) → Hook (CTA)**

Best for: Sales emails, promotional emails

| Element | Purpose | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Star | Big attention-grabber | Surprising fact, bold claim |
| Chain | Logical reasons | Benefits, proof, explanation |
| Hook | Compelling CTA | Irresistible call to action |

---

### SOAP
**Subject → Opening → Argument → Push**

Best for: Persuasive emails, objection handling

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Subject | Curiosity-driven open |
| Opening | Connect emotionally |
| Argument | Logical case |
| Push | Clear CTA |

---

## Framework Selection Guide

| Situation | Recommended Framework |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Quick landing page | AIDA |
| Pain-focused audience | PAS |
| Long sales page | PASTOR |
| Case study/testimonial | BAB |
| Direct response | 4Ps |
| Social post | HCEA |
| Brand messaging | StoryBrand |
| Newsletter | 1-2-3 |
| Sales email | Star-Chain-Hook |
| Educational content | QUEST |

---

## Framework Combinations

Complex pieces often combine frameworks:

| Page Type | Combination |
|-----------|-------------|
| Sales page | PASTOR (structure) + BAB (testimonials) |
| Landing page | AIDA (flow) + PAS (problem section) |
| Email sequence | Day 1: HCEA → Day 3: PAS → Day 5: PASTOR |
